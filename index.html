Rules
Since we're a commity, we have to follow some rules to keep everything under control. They are -
  1. Try to be active as much as you can.
  2. Respect everyone!(Mostly female members).
  3. We do not allow temporary players, hoppers and rushers.
  4. Try to use your both attacks in war and get 6 stars.
  5. Donate for donation.
  6. Ask before taking any action.
  7. Try to use mirror attack in war.
  8. Don't ask for promotion, you'll be promoted if you're qualified.
  9. Do not use bad words! (If you use bad words, you will get demoted and if you do it again you will get kicked)
  10. Try to donate right troops! If you donate wrong troop ,you may have the risk of kick or demotion.
  11. Respect the troops that you have gotten from your clan member.
  12. Don't quarrel with each other.
  13. Friendly Challenge per day , number of a single member- 12 per day. If you cross you might have the risk of demotion . In this case kick is not allowed.
  14. Must visit godofwarcoc.github.io when leader sends a clan mail. In this case, 1st,2nd,3rd time demotions are not allowed but after third warning you may get demoted ! In this case , kick is not allowed!

War Rules
You can not win a war wihout following the commands of your commander. At GoW, Dark Hawk is your commander of war. He has experience of winning about 542 war stars. Follow his commands to win a war. Additional instructions by Dark Hawk :
  1. Make your war base stronger.
  2. Scout enemy war base more and more and plan your attack strategy.
  3. Your first attack is mirror attack.

    4. Q: What is mirror attack?
    A: Mirror attack is attacking the opponent who is standing exactly in front of you. Let me give you an example to make it clear. Suppose your position in war is No.12 and if you attack enemy No.12 , it will be called mirror attack.

  4. Wait for second attack instruction from your commander.

  6. Don't spoil others attack.
  7. You don't need to donate in war base clan castle. Your commander has the idea.
  8. No attack will cause no participation in upcoming two wars.
